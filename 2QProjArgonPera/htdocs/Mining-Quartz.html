Quartz

A clear crystal commonly found in caves and mines.

Quartz is a Mineral that can be found by foraging in The Mines and in Skull Cavern. It can also occasionally be found in Garbage Cans. Stone Golems have a 10% chance to drop Quartz when slain. A Ghostfish Fish Pond may produce 1 Quartz when the population reaches 3, and 3 Quartz when the population reaches 9.

Quartz can be destroyed by bombs.

Quartz can be used along with 1 coal in a furnace to produce Refined Quartz, taking 1.5 in-game hours to smelt.
