Fish Shop

“If the local fishing scene got a bit more lively, I might expand the shop's stock.”-Willy

The Fish Shop is the home of Willy. It's located on the docks of The Beach south of Pelican Town. If the player has not read the letter from Willy sent on Spring 2, then the shop cannot be entered. Although the door will unlock on Saturdays, the shop will be closed unless it is raining.

Willy sells a variety of fishing-related supplies, including rods, bait, tackle, fish tanks, and crab pots. More goods become available as the player increases their fishing skill. These goods can be purchased as soon as the appropriate skill level is achieved, without needing to wait until the next day.

Information
Open Hours: 9am to 5pm
Closed: Non-rainy Saturdays (Building Open, no sales)
Occupants:
Willy
