Description of the Game

Stardew Valley is an open-ended country-life RPG! You've gotten tired of your boring and repetative city life, which means only one thing, it's time to open up Grandpa's letter! In it you find the deed to your grandpa's farm and a note encouraging you to leave your life behind to go live there. Armed with hand-me-down tools and a few coins, you set out to begin your new life. Can you learn to live off the land and turn these overgrown fields into a thriving home? It won’t be easy. Ever since Joja Corporation came to town, the old ways of life have all but disappeared. The community center, once the town’s most vibrant hub of activity, now lies in ruin. But the valley seems full of good opportunities. With a bit of dedication and a lot of hard work, you might just be the one to restore Stardew Valley to greatness!
